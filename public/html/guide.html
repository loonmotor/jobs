Guide

Here are a few guidelines to get you started in no time.

Before you have signed up / signed in, you will have access to the following use cases :

  • You can browse for jobs.
  • You can search for jobs.
  • You can sign up.
  • You can sign in.

Once you have signed up / signed in, you will have access to the following use cases :

  • You can be a recruiter and/or applicant.
  • As a recruiter :
    • You can create a company. A company is required if you would like to create a job.
    • You can create a job. Make sure you have created at least one company beforehand.
    • You can edit and/or remove your companies. Removing a company will remove all its associated jobs too.
    • You can edit and/or remove the jobs offered by your companies.
    • You can archive the jobs offered by your companies.
    • You can unarchive the jobs offered by your companies.
    • You can view applicants that are interested in the jobs offered by your companies.
  • As an applicant :
    • You can create a profile. A profile is required if you would like to submit your interest to a job.
    • You can edit your profile.
    • You can browse for jobs offered and submit your interests to them.
    • You can search for jobs.
    • You can view jobs that you are interested in.
    • You can remove interests from jobs that you were previously interested in.
